Course Terrain Tool

Create your OPCD course terrain in one click

Download Beta
Available for Windows and Mac

Course Terrain Tool is an open source tool to find and convert raw lidar elevation data to raw unity terrain data. Specifically for use in the OPCD course design process.

How it works

  1. Define your inner (and optional outer) area of your course on the map
  2. Search the area for free lidar data sources
  3. Configure your resolution settings
  4. Sit back and watch the magic happen!
